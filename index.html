Media and My Data

Ruben Verborgh

Ghent University – imec

What do you do when
you have all the data
in the world?

Solid redefines
the relation between
people, apps, and data.

People can choose where they store
every single piece of data they produce.

People give apps and other people access
to very specific parts of their data.

Separating app and storage competition
drives permissionless innovation.

There’s not going to be
less data.

There will be more.
